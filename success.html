What is Quinnipiac Competition?

Quinnipiac is a programming competition. In this competition, students will work in groups of 3 to solve 6-9 problems in 3 hours.

What is GE Case Study?

It is a (competition)/(case study) hosted by General Electric. Students will have over a month to do a project on given topic. In 2014 the assignment was Tetris AI.

Weekly Programming Challenge:

Multiples of 3 and 5

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23. Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.
